# OMEGA Architecture

OMEGA is a provider-independent trading operating system that improves decision context around SignalFlow through accumulated knowledge, market intelligence, analytics, and experience.

OMEGA does not attempt to make more decisions. OMEGA attempts to make better understood, better governed, and better validated decisions.

## Core Loop

```text
Knowledge
↓
Market Intelligence
↓
AI
↓
Decision Intelligence
↓
SignalFlow
↓
Paper Trading
↓
Analytics
↓
Experience
↓
Knowledge
```

## Permanent OMEGA Rule

SignalFlow is the sole decision authority.

All other systems contribute context, observations, analytics, experience, knowledge, explanations, or validation.

Parallel decision systems are prohibited.

## Context Rule

- Knowledge provides context.
- Market Intelligence provides context.
- AI provides reasoning support.
- Decision Intelligence explains context.
- SignalFlow makes decisions.
- Paper Trading validates decisions.
- Analytics measures behavior.
- Experience captures lessons.
- TradingView provides optional observations and validation.

## Ownership Boundaries

- SignalFlow owns decisions and orchestration.
- Decision Intelligence owns context and explanations.
- TradingView owns observations and validation only.
- Analytics owns measurement and insight only.
- Experience owns lessons and patterns only.
- Knowledge owns reusable context only.
